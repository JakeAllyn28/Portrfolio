# Multiplayer Vehicle Combat Simulation

**Context:** Personal Project  
**Stack:** Unity, C#, networking  
**Focus:** Vehicle Physics, Multiplayer Systems  

---

## Overview

Developed a multiplayer vehicle combat prototype featuring tanks, helicopters, and aircraft with physics-based movement and combat systems.

The project focused on combining realistic vehicle behavior with gameplay-driven mechanics.

---

## Problem

- Needed to support multiple vehicle types with distinct control models  
- Required stable multiplayer behavior with physics-driven systems  
- Gameplay systems (abilities, combat) needed to integrate with physics  

---

## Core Systems

- Physics-based vehicle movement for ground and air vehicles  
- Multiplayer architecture supporting peer-to-peer gameplay  
- AI-controlled enemies with navigation and combat behaviors  

---

## Customization & Gameplay

- Modular vehicle customization system  
- Ability-based combat system layered on top of physics  
- Multiple vehicle classes with unique handling characteristics  

---

## Technical Details

- Custom vehicle physics implementations in Unity  
- Network synchronization for player-controlled entities  
- AI behavior systems integrated with navigation and combat  

---

## Results / Impact

- Demonstrated integration of physics, multiplayer, and gameplay systems  
- Provided a sandbox for experimenting with vehicle dynamics  
- Informed future approaches to vehicle system design
