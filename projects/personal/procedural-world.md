# Procedural World & Dungeon Generation

**Context:** Personal Project  
**Stack:** Unity, C#  
**Focus:** Procedural Generation, Game Systems  

---

## Overview

Developed procedural systems for generating terrain, points of interest (POIs), and dungeon layouts to create dynamic, replayable environments.

---

## Problem

- Needed large, varied environments without manual design  
- Required structure to guide player exploration  
- AI and gameplay systems needed consistent world logic  

---

## Terrain & World Generation

- Generated terrain with structured placement of major POIs  
- Connected POIs via road networks to guide player movement  
- Balanced randomness with intentional layout design  

---

## POI System

### Major POIs
- Large, objective-driven locations  
- Designed to anchor gameplay progression  

### Micro POIs

- Smaller locations distributed throughout the world  
- Used to:
  - Reward exploration  
  - Populate empty space  
  - Support AI behaviors  
  - Provide loot opportunities  

---

## AI & Gameplay Integration

- Used POIs as anchors for AI patrol routes  
- Integrated with loot spawning systems  
- Provided structure for mission and objective systems  

---

## Technical Details

- Rule-based generation system  
- Layered approach to world building (terrain → POIs → detail)  
- Data-driven configuration for flexibility  

---

## Results / Impact

- Created dynamic, replayable environments  
- Balanced exploration and guided gameplay  
- Enabled integration of world generation with AI and systems
